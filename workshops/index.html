What the day covers

Morning

Location scouting and light planning. Camera and flash setup. Theory session on rear curtain sync, motion blur, and exposure for blue hour. All gear provided where needed.

Blue Hour

The main event. Rear curtain streak shots, flash-lit portraits, and long-exposure mountain road work — during the 20-minute window after sunset where everything comes together.

Lightroom Classic edit session on your shots from the day. Preset pack and setup diagrams included in your workshop materials.
